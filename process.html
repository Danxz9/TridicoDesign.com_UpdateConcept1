Process

From rough idea to finished product without guesswork.

The process page is designed to reduce customer uncertainty. It explains what the customer needs to provide and how Tridico moves from design to production.

Start Step 1
01

Discovery

Project type, business goal, location, deadline, materials, vehicle details, measurements, and existing artwork are collected.

02

Design & Proof

Concepts are built around visibility, brand consistency, real-world size, legibility, and production requirements.

03

Production

Approved work moves into print, fabrication, finishing, cutting, lamination, or preparation for installation.

04

Installation / Delivery

Final materials are installed, delivered, or handed off with the right expectations for care and use.

What customers should prepare

Better inputs create better outcomes.

This checklist helps the quote and design process move faster.

For wraps

Vehicle year, make, model, photos, VIN/template if available, existing logo, desired coverage, and timeline.

For signage

Install location photos, measurements, mounting surface, indoor/outdoor use, desired visibility distance, and permit constraints if any.

For print

Quantity, size, paper/material preference, deadline, finished use, artwork files, and mailing/folding needs if applicable.

For branding

Existing logo files, business goals, audience, examples, required applications, color preferences, and brand pain points.
