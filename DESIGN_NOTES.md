# Design Notes and Replacement Map

## Design formula

- Tridico brand identity: black, yellow, red, supplied TD mark, and the exact motto “Welcome to integrity, Welcome to Tridico.”
- Apple influence: premium spacing, clean hero sections, high restraint, and short confident copy.
- Google influence: approachable service cards, scannable navigation, and easy user flow.
- NVIDIA influence: dark, high-impact proof sections and confident technical/production framing.
- TKO-style influence: quote-focused header, phone-forward contact, art upload path, and wrap/sign/product-service organization.
- Tridico-specific correction: the brand/logo/color/motto system is Tridico, not Apple/Google/NVIDIA/TKO.

## Header strategy

Primary nav: Work, Services, Industries, Process, About, Contact.  
Utility actions: Phone, Upload Artwork, Get Quote.  
Mobile priority: Work, Services, Vehicle Wraps, Signs & Banners, Branding, Printing, Industries, Process, Upload, Contact, Quote.
